Ube Halaya

Description:
Ube Halaya, also known as purple yam jam, is a vibrant and delicious Filipino dessert made from purple yam, coconut milk, and condensed milk. The dessert has a distinctive purple color and a rich, creamy, and sweet flavor. Ube Halaya is often served during special occasions and is a delightful treat that captures the essence of Filipino cuisine.

Recipe for Ube Halaya:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mashed purple yam (ube)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) condensed milk
  • 1 can (13.5 ounces) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, combine the mashed purple yam, condensed milk, coconut milk, and sugar.
  2. Cook the mixture over low heat while continuously stirring until it thickens and the color becomes more vibrant (approximately 20-30 minutes).
  3. Add the butter and continue cooking and stirring until the mixture becomes thick and easily separates from the pan (approximately 15-20 minutes).
  4. Transfer the Ube Halaya to a greased dish or mold and flatten the top with a spatula.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate for a few hours to set.
  6. Slice, serve, and savor the delightful taste of Ube Halaya!

Indulge in the vibrant and sweet flavors of Ube Halaya, a Filipino dessert that's as delightful as it is colorful!
